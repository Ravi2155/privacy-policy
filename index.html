Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how the Bhagavad Gita App ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide you with the best experience:

  • Language preferences - Stored locally on your device
  • Theme preferences - Stored locally on your device
  • App usage data - Anonymized analytics data through Firebase Analytics

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email, or location without your explicit consent.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Personalize your reading experience
  • Remember your language and theme preferences
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Provide better user experience
  • Understand app usage patterns (anonymized)

3. Data Storage

Your preferences are stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms. We do not transmit your personal data to external servers unless you explicitly submit feedback or report issues through the app's feedback feature.

4. Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Analytics - For understanding app usage and improving user experience (anonymized data)
  • Firebase Crashlytics - For identifying and fixing app crashes
  • Firebase Remote Config - For managing app features and configurations
  • Google Services - For app distribution through Google Play Store

These services have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information. You can review Google's privacy policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored preferences (through app settings)
  • Delete your app data by uninstalling the app
  • Opt-out of analytics data collection (disable in app settings if available)
  • Request information about data we hold

6. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
